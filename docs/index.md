# Home

Welcome to the CASA theoretical knowledge hub. Everything you need to know regarding Australian ATS phraseology and Standard Operating Procedures can be found here for use on the ATC24 network and even possibly VATSIM. Note the contents of this site are for educational purposes only and should not be used within the real world. 

A majority of information within this site is taken from the VATSIM Australia Pacific division's SOPs and training material and the Air Services Australia AIP book and Manual of Air Traffic Services.
